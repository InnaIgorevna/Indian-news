From Rs 3,000 to $1,000 a Month. How Mumbai Boy Discovered a New Money Making Method

Mumbai,
PTI
Mumbai,

On October 5, the rupee had settled at 74.44 against the U.S. dollar

Kumar Singh from Mumbai went from an ordinary office boy’s salary to $1,000 a month. Read his exclusive interview.

I lost my father at an early age. My mom was left alone with two children, so even before I went to school, I started distributing newspapers in the streets of Mumbai. However, my mother still had to work two jobs, and my sister and I hardly saw her.

In high school, I started thinking about getting a job that would support my family. I dreamed of becoming an IT specialist, but after completing Class 10 I started working as an office boy for 3,000 rupees a month.

I still wanted to lift my family out of poverty. I was working in the office for 2 years, the staff there hardly changed. One day one of them came wearing a fancy watch. At that time no one paid attention to that, although everyone understood that it was an expensive purchase.

Over time, the guy got other expensive accessories. One day he drove up to the office building in a new car. They began to suspect him of working for competitors, but no evidence was found and then they did not fire him.

They fired him a month later. It turned out that during working hours he was not doing his tasks, but was watching some kind of diagrams on his office computer. As it turned out, thanks to those diagrams he got additional money for a watch and a car.

The boss was furious. He was shouting and poking his finger at the monitor, and on the screen I saw the logo of a trading platform.

I got home and typed the platform’s name into Google. I was immediately bombarded with YouTube videos — How to Win Every Trade with Proof, 99% Accurate Strategy, Trading App Legal In India and others. I got interested and started watching videos one by one.

There were a lot of videos, and my desire to try trading was growing. I have chosen the most understandable strategy. I registered on the platform’s official website and started trading.

START TRADING

The whole day I continued trading on a demo account. I made mistakes, but I didn't give up. The guys in the video said it was okay to make mistakes while you’re training. When the number of correct predictions increased, I deposited a real account with my own $10.

I made my first real $12 in an hour. I was trading in my spare time all week and got a pretty good amount of money. I was excited and showed the money to my mother. She broke into tears.

In 2020, the pandemic hit the world. I had to give up my office work for a while, but now I make about $1,000 a month trading.

My mother, sister and I stayed at home during the pandemic and thanks to that money we got everything we wanted.

Now I have started saving money again. My next dream is to buy a bigger house. I finally became a real man and the head of my family.
